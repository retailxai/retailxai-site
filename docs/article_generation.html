Article Generation

RetailXAI transforms raw transcripts and content into professional, publication-ready articles using advanced AI models.

Writing Styles

Choose from multiple writing styles to match your publication needs:

  • Analytical: Data-driven analysis with charts and metrics
  • Narrative: Storytelling approach with engaging prose
    • Data-Driven: Heavy focus on statistics and quantitative analysis
  • Opinion: Editorial style with clear viewpoints

AI Models

RetailXAI supports multiple AI models:
